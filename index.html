Academic Experience

Researcher

2018 - Present

IIT Delhi, New Delhi, India

  • As part of my PhD research at IIT Delhi, I am working on developing a Non-Intrusive Load Monitoring (NILM) framework which helps the power utility companies to know their consumers' appliance usage patterns by using the data obtained from their smart metres. NILM results can assist in better-implementing demand side management (DSM) measures, clustering consumers, reducing power distribution losses and improving the economic health of distribution companies (DISCOMs). The framework is based on the theory of Markov models, non-linear optimization, time series analysis and other machine learning-related concepts.
  • I am also developing the market clearing framework for the power distribution system, integrated with renewable energy resources, electric vehicles and batteries.
